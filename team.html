Electronics

The Electronics group manage all the ROVs electrical work. This includes designing the complete hardware system, from scratch, to ensure the ROV is fit to perform the tasks of the competition. Our work range from designing custome PCB's to assembling the finished system - and troubleshooting it all when it does not work! The Electronics team combine knowledge and creativity to effectively solve our problems. Optimizing the solutions we reach follows as a natural part of work.

We work in close relations to both the Mechanical- and the Controller groups.

Kristian Bortne

Chief Mechanical
Hometown : Måløy

Mechanical

The mechanical group is responsible for designing a waterproof ROV frame construction. This includes 3d-modelling of our wanted design, designing own thrusters, integrating the thrusters and cameras to the design and production of buoyancy elements. After the design phase, it is our job to coordinate the manufacturing of all the different pieces.

The mechanical group is also responsible for the design and production of a capable manipulator arm that can pick up and handle objects. The arm is an essential and critical part of the ROV allowing it to complete tasks assigned in the MATE competition.
